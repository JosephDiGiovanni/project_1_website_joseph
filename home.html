Space Exploration

For ages, humans have wondered what lies beyond the stars. What is in the massive, dark abyss we call the universe. What else is out there? Will we have the technology to find out? These have all recently been very popular questions. We humans aren't meant to stay on Earth forever. Eventually, we will have to leave and move on to the next planet.

NASA

NASA (the National Aeronautics and Space Administration) is a fedral agency and a part of the executive branch devoted to space.

NASA has been around for 58 years, and has been a main contributer to the study of astonomy, astrology, aerospace engineering, and astrophysics. NASA is one of the most significant scientific agencies in the the world. They've sent rovers like Pathfinder, Spirit, and Opportunity to Mars. With the Apollo 11 mission, Neil Armstrong walked on the moon. With the Hubble Telescope and the Kepler Astronnomical Observatory, we have been able to see images of planets in other star systems.
