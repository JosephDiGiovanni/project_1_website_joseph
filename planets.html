Planets

Scientists have been on the lookout for potentially habitable planets for if we ever had to leave Earth. NASA is currently using the Kepler Space Observatory to locate Earth-like planets in the event that Earth became uninhabitable. So far, Kepler has discovered 1,284 planets. These include Kepler 452b, Kepler 186f, and Kepler 22b. These are all planets that could potentially sustain human life and potentially alien life.
